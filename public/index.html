Worldwide Web

The Journey of the Web

Starting from the Matrix times, where the audience sees only a black screen with green text is unimaginable currently today. How it quickly evolved within a matter of two decades is remarkable within itself. Going through dial up phases to applications on the first smart phone in a short amount of time proves the success of the new technology era.

The Future of the Web

My visions of the web would be through the eyes of Virtual Reality. Where we will put on a pair of goggles and surf through the web using sensory gloves. Children will be able to gain exceptional educational experiences through using virtual reality. Our government could create peaceful interactions with others from around the world through a Skype session in the VR space. Cultures would be more transparent to individuals who do not have the economic means to experience the countries they choose to visit.
